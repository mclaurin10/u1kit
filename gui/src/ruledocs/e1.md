## E1 — Strut/wall line count at current scale

Parses object dimensions, estimates thinnest feature, and warns when `thinnest_feature ÷ outer_wall_line_width < 3`. Flags gap-fill territory where walls either merge or print hollow. No fix — the geometry choice is yours; rule suggests dropping `line_width` or accepting perimeter-only walls.

**Severity:** warn.

**Why this matters:** three perimeters is the minimum for walls to self-support during printing. Below that, Orca falls back to gap-fill — which looks fine in the slicer preview but produces surface artifacts or outright hollows in the print.
