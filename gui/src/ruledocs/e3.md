## E3 — Prime tower stability at small plate scales

If plate footprint's min dimension is under 120 mm AND `prime_tower_brim_width` is under 5 mm AND a prime tower is in use, suggests bumping the brim. Auto-fix is opt-in via preset option `e3_auto_bump: true` — default conservative, enabled in `peba-safe` / `plus-peba-multi`.

**Severity:** warn.

**Why this matters:** the prime tower is a tall, thin structure that wants to tip over during toolchanges. A wider brim gives it a broader base at the cost of some filament and time. The opt-in gate exists because on flexibles the cost is worth it; on rigids with a wide plate it often isn't.
