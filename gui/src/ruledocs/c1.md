## C1 — Bed temperature conflict

When multiple filaments share a plate with differing `hot_plate_temp` or `textured_plate_temp`, auto-fix picks the lowest common value and applies it to the corresponding `curr_bed_type` temp field. The conflict is surfaced in the report so you know what was reconciled.

**Severity:** fail — mismatched bed temps trigger Print Preprocessing errors.

**Why this matters:** the bed can only hold one temperature at a time. Picking the lowest common value avoids delamination on the cooler-loving filaments while keeping adhesion on the hotter-loving ones, at the cost of slightly longer first-layer bonding for the latter.
