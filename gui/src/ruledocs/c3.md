## C3 — Minimum cooling time mismatch

Takes the maximum `slow_down_layer_time` across used filaments on the plate (most conservative wins). PEBA's 12 s trumps PLA's 4 s.

**Severity:** warn — mixing a filament that needs longer minimum cooling with one that doesn't can over-cool and under-adhere the latter.

**Why this matters:** small layers on hot-running filaments need extra seconds for the nozzle to move away. The U1's cooling fan runs continuously, so choosing the larger minimum is the safe default — at the cost of some total print time.
