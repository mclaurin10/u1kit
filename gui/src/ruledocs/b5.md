## B5 — Flexible filament used as its own support

If `support_filament` and `support_interface_filament` both point to a flexible (TPU/PEBA), auto-fix reassigns support to a rigid alternative (prefers PLA, falls back to PETG/ABS/ASA/PC). If no rigid filament is present, emits a warn only.

**Severity:** warn — flexible-on-flexible support is mechanically brittle and often tears apart.

**Why this matters:** supports in flexibles don't hold shape — the interface layer squishes under print force, and you end up with sag, spaghetti, or both. A rigid support layer gives the flexible top a clean parting plane.
