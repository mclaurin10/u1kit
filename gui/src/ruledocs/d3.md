## D3 — 1:1 alternation cost awareness

When `mixed_filament_definitions` contains blends with 1:1 ratios, surfaces an estimated toolchange count (`layer_count × #blends_with_50_percent`). Informational only — no fix.

**Severity:** info.

**Why this matters:** 1:1 blends force one toolchange per layer per blend. On a 200-layer print with two 1:1 blends, that's 400 extra toolchanges — often hours of print time. If cycle time matters and the blend is cosmetic, consider a 70/30 or stripe ratio instead.
