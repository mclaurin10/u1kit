## A3 — Bambu AMS G-code macros present

Scans `machine_start_gcode`, `machine_end_gcode`, `change_filament_gcode`, and `layer_change_gcode` for Bambu AMS syntax (`M620`/`M621`/`M623`), strips it, and replaces it with the U1-equivalent toolchange sequence from `u1kit/data/u1_toolchange.gcode`.

**Severity:** fail — Bambu AMS commands hang the U1's firmware.

**Why this matters:** Makerworld exports embed the AMS orchestration directly in the start/toolchange G-code. The U1's four-tool toolchanger uses a different command vocabulary and mechanical routine; the embedded AMS codes are not just no-ops — they can put the machine in an unexpected state.
