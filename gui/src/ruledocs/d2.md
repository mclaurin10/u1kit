## D2 — Z-hop magnitude vs layer height

Flags when Z-hop in `z_hop` or `filament_z_hop` is ≥ 5× layer height. At 0.04 mm layer height with a 0.2 mm hop, that's the ~37-layer catastrophe; at 0.2 mm it's benign. Auto-fix caps Z-hop at `max(1.5 mm, 4 × layer_height)` and zeros `filament_z_hop` so it doesn't override.

**Severity:** warn.

**Why this matters:** Z-hop protects a print from dragging during travels, but a hop too large for the layer height can pop the head above tall features and into a collision. The cap gives enough clearance for normal travels without entering collision territory.
