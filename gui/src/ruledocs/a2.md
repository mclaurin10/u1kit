## A2 — Printer profile not U1

Rewrites `printer_settings_id`, `printer_model`, bed shape, and `machine_max_*` kinematics to Snapmaker U1 values. Source of truth: the bundled `u1_printer_reference.json`.

**Severity:** fail — without a U1 profile, Snapmaker Orca refuses to open the file.

**Why this matters:** this is the first wall any Bambu/Makerworld import hits. The U1's kinematics, print volume, extruder layout, and startup sequence differ enough from any other printer that a mismatched profile produces either a rejection or unpredictable prints.
