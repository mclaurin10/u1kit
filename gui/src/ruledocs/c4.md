## C4 — Fan speed range conflict

Surfaces per-filament fan ranges. No single fix — per-filament fan settings apply automatically at toolchange on the U1, so the ranges are informational rather than corrective.

**Severity:** info.

**Why this matters:** when you see wildly different fan ranges across used filaments, that's your signal to verify the toolchange-time fan-speed swap is actually working on your machine. It also explains why "the same file printed differently" between identical-looking slicer setups.
