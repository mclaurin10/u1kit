## C2 — First-layer bed temp conflict

Same reconciliation logic as C1 but applied to first-layer bed temps. Additional rule: if a textured-PEI plate is selected and any filament specifies >65 °C for the first layer, cap at 65 °C (safe textured-PEI range).

**Severity:** fail — over-temp first layer on PEI textured plates damages the coating over time.

**Why this matters:** textured PEI's adhesion peaks at a narrower temperature band than smooth PEI. Going hotter than ~65 °C on the first layer doesn't improve adhesion and cumulatively degrades the plate. The 65 °C cap is conservative; tune it in `peba-safe.yaml` if your plate tolerates more.
